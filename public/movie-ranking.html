The Best Movies According to Kevin

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Spider-man: Across the Spiderverse

A fantastic 3d-animated movie utilizing multiple animation styles and techniques to create a unique experience.

Equalizer 3

An action packed movie with a pretty decent story in my opinion

Transformers: Rise of the Beasts

A solid take on an old animated series from the 1990s. Had me on the edge of my seat from start to finish.
